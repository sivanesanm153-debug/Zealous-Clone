Our Proven Process

From absolute concept modeling up to high-fidelity web integrations in five simple steps.

Step 01

Discovery & Intake

We analyze blueprints, reference photos, sizing sheets, or CAD files to define the modeling targets, lighting style, and export formats.

Step 02

Topology & Geometry

Our modeling team builds the mesh geometry. We optimize poly counts so assets load lightning-fast on mobile browsers or web stores.

Step 03

Material & Shader Design

We texture models using PBR (Physically Based Rendering) standards, configuring leather grain, brushed metals, glass refractivity, and light bounces.

Step 04

High-Fidelity QA Check

Every rendering passes through multi-tier review, checking against source materials for sizing precision and pixel-perfect lighting setups.

Step 05

Final Handoff & Launch

Assets are delivered in requested formats (.glb, .usdz, .obj, high-res PNG, MP4 loop) along with ready-to-paste embed codes.
