What is a Virtual Showroom?

A virtual showroom is an interactive spatial environment replicating a real physical store. It allows users to walk through collections, click on active product hotspots, and test custom configurator variables (materials, wood finish, colorways) before purchase.

At Vertex XR, we engineer multi-room virtual spaces that integrate with e-commerce payment APIs. We implement optimized asset pipelines to ensure your spatial storefront loads instantly on mobile, tablet, and VR web browsers alike.
