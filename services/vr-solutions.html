What is VR Solutions?

Virtual Reality (VR) immerses users in complete, digitally-constructed environments. By simulating actual scales and hand interactions, VR enables users to practice hazard management, walk through pre-construction structures, and experience spatial depth naturally.

At Vertex XR, we build custom VR applications using Unity, Unreal, and WebXR technologies. We focus on low-latency interactions, multi-user sync, and realistic physics response to make training scenarios transfer directly to real-world execution.
