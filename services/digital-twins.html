What is a Digital Twin?

A digital twin is a dynamic 3D model of a physical object, device, or facility, updated from real-time database feeds. By mirroring operational telemetry, digital twins let engineers trace cycles, test stressors, and isolate issues before physical failures occur.

At Vertex XR, we connect high-fidelity WebGL structures with your API systems. Replicating layout setups, assembly lines, or engine interiors, we map heat values, friction, and fluid flows straight onto the active 3D components.

Live WebSocket Telemetry

We configure sub-second pipelines linking WebGL components to MQTT or WebSocket brokers. As sensor measurements change, the corresponding 3D geometries adapt instantly, updating gauges and heatmaps in real-time.

Stress-Testing Sandboxes

Model predictive paths inside the digital twin. Manipulate virtual speed variables, heat, or volume inputs to stress-test your machinery setup under extreme conditions safely before applying physical shifts.
