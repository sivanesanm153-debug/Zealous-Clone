What is Gaming Assets?

Gaming assets represent the 3D models, textures, characters, and environmental props that construct game worlds. Since games calculate lighting and geometry in real-time, assets must contain clean quad topologies, correct pivot offsets, and optimized material groupings to run at high framerates.

At Vertex XR, we develop engine-compatible game art. We specialize in baking details from high-polygon sculpts down to lightweight low-poly meshes, uv mapping, and painting custom PBR maps in Substance Painter.

Normal & Occlusion Baking

Clean Quad Retopology

We build loops that flow along organic joints, elbows, and facial muscles. This clean topology prevents texture stretching and geometry clipping when character models are rigged and animated.

Engine-Ready Out-Of-The-Box

Eliminate import errors. We deliver models configured with unified coordinate scales, clean pivot placements, and matching material channels, loading cleanly inside Unreal Engine, Unity, and WebGL.
