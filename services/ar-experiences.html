What is AR Experiences?

Augmented Reality overlays 3D geometry onto a viewer's physical room feed. By connecting products with mobile camera systems, AR lets online shoppers view furniture alignments, size items against real spaces, or check colors before placing an order.

We build lightweight, cross-device WebXR solutions. Buyers simply scan a QR code or tap a button—no App Store downloads are required. The browser activates local AR rendering instantly, placing models in real space with photorealistic light matching.

App-Less WebXR Delivery

Eliminate download friction. Our AR viewers load directly inside Safari and Chrome on iOS and Android. They use native WebXR parameters or Apple QuickLook overlays so experiences launch in under two seconds.

Physics-Based Lighting & Scale

Our models include coordinate configurations that match real physical proportions. Once placed, smart scripts query the user's camera feed to estimate room lighting, casting soft, matched shadows onto the floor.
