What is 3D Animation?

3D animation is the process of generating three-dimensional moving images in a digital environment. It is the ultimate tool for explaining complex engineering features, highlighting structural details, and telling visual stories that flat media cannot capture.

At Vertex XR, we animate products with clean physical motion. We specialize in assemblies, rotational spins, and explainer simulations that show inside-out views of hardware, consumer goods, and fashion items.

Physics-Based Motion Dynamics

We build our animations using true physics parameters. Gravity, elasticity, rotational speeds, and inertia are calculated so that assemblies fit together naturally, avoiding jerky, unrealistic manual keyframes.

Clean X-Ray & Cutaway Views

Show what lies beneath the surface. Our animation pipeline handles camera clipping, material opacity fades, and assembly paths so viewers see inside motors, batteries, or textiles without losing context.
