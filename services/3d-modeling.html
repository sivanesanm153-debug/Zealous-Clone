What is 3D Modeling?

3D modeling is the process of developing a mathematical representation of any three-dimensional surface of an object. In modern e-commerce and interactive spaces, it is the foundation of every immersive experience. By translating product dimensions, CAD files, or physical references into perfect digital geometry, it lets clients inspect products from every angle.

At Vertex XR, we specialize in high-quality 3D asset pipelines. We ensure clean edge-loops, lightweight file sizes (under 3MB for WebGL), and physics-based rendering (PBR) textures that look identical to physical items in real-world lighting.

Modeling Capabilities

We cover the entire asset lifecycle from raw CAD conversion to real-time mobile visualizer deployment.

Hard Surface Modeling

Highly precise, mathematical geometry built for electronics, furniture, automotive, and industrial machinery.

Organic Sculpting

High-polygon detail capturing organic structures, clothing folds, footwear textures, and stylized brand assets.

Web & AR Optimization

Polygon reduction, mesh decimation, and Draco/KTX2 texture compression to load assets in under 2 seconds on mobile.

CAD to Web Conversion

Direct conversion of engineering files (STEP, IGES, SolidWorks, Rhino) to web-friendly formats without losing details.

Physics-Based Rendering (PBR) Textures

We map real-world materials (metals, glass, leathers, fabrics) onto 3D meshes using industry-standard roughness, metalness, and normal maps. This creates physical tactile depth and lets the surface react dynamically to change in scene lighting.

Draco Compression for Fast Loads

No user wants to wait for a 50MB model. We utilize Google Draco and glTF-pipeline algorithms to compress geometries and textures down to less than 2MB, reducing loading latency on mobile browsers while retaining 98% of the visual detail.
